about us

Rope Zone has come a long way relatively short time form as a Provider and Supplier of rope access services. It has alleviated the Ropezone team to be recognized locally and overseas. We are ready to provide any demonstration to cater to our client's needs and to provide training as required.

Working at Height and Rope access techniques, offer a cost-effective alternative to conventional access system (scaffolding, cranes, cradles, and cherry picker). It has proven in over 10 years the successful application of rope access techniques used offshore and onshore, on buildings, steel structures, bridges, dams, masts, refiners, platforms, etc. We prepare our personnel at every level to be carefully selected and undergo a comprehensive induction and training program.

To ensure that, each and every job is carried out effectively and efficiently well trained staff who have to undergone comprehensive induction and training program are specially selected
